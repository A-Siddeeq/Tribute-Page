Biography

Mallam Nasir Ahmad El-Rufai Born 16th February 1960 is a Nigerian Politician, innovator and visionary leader. He is the incumbent and 22nd Executive Governor of Kaduna State. El-Rufai, as he is commonly called had served as the Director of the BPE and the Minister of the Federal Capital Territory where he made significant contributions to the Nation that till today, his accomplihments are still present.
Since sworn in as governor of Kaduna State on 29th May 2015, the improvement his administration brought about has made notable changes in the state, including but not limited to:

  • Increase in IGR from just N11.7bn in 2015 to N50.77bn in 2020
  • Introduced the kaduna Urban Renewal Project which foresaw(forsees) the Urbanisation of various infrastructure in the state
  • Launched the school feeding programme which provides a free meal to students per day
  • Renovated various schools, sports centers and facilities to higher levels
  • Adopted the TSA Policy

Infact, all the notable achievements can not be listed because of the vastness. Nasir El-Rufai is an inspiration to many due to his notable achievements
